その時代が放つ
真の輝きを求めて

金沢は金箔国内生産量の約9割以上を占めています。
金沢箔は、金箔の製造に適した北陸の気候風土と
この地で育った箔職人の強い情熱によって、その製法や伝統が受け継がれ、国宝や重要文化財の修復などに用いられています。

私ども株式会社中村製箔所は、金沢箔の四代続く製造元として、金沢箔の製法と、その伝統を色濃く受け継いでまいりました。
日本を誇る、金箔の町として、この伝統を守り、次世代へと伝えながら、これからも、その時代の輝きを作り続けてまいります。

Continuing to create the true brilliance of past eras

Kanazawa is responsible for over 90% of the gold leaf produced in Japan. In particular, the production methods and traditions of "Kanazawa-haku (Kanazawa gold leaf)" have been passed down from one generation to the next through the climate and natural features of the Hokuriku region which are well adapted to the production of gold leaf as well as the intense passion of craftsmen who grew up in Kanazawa. Kanazawa-haku is routinely used in the restoration of Japan's national treasures and important cultural properties.
Nakamura Seihakusho, Inc. has been manufacturing Kanazawa-haku for four generations and we have inherited the key production methods and traditions of Kanazawa-haku. As the face of the city of gold leaf that is the pride of Japan, we will safeguard this tradition and pass it on to future generations while continuing to create the brilliance of past eras.
